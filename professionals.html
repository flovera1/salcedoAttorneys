  • Meet our team.


    With headquarters in Miami, Florida, and capabilities that extend throughout Latin America, Salcedo Attorneys at Law P.A. offers its unique brand of domestic and international legal counsel to a select group of clients.
    Salcedo attorneys focus their practice on international-domestic transactions, corporate and international tax planning, distribution agreements, real estate transactions and business immigration law, covering industries that include food and beverage, oil and energy, banking, real estate, and international courier and money services.

    Founder Jorge Salcedo H. focuses his practice on domestic-international acquisitions, distribution and production agreements, real estate transactions,business immigration planning, and corporate and tax planning for foreign investors with assets in the United States.

    Prior to founding Salcedo Attorneys at Law P.A., he was a member of one the largest multinational law firms in the State of Florida. He has represented fortune 500 companies, private equity funds, family offices and high net-worth individuals in a broad array of business transactions.

    Mr. Salcedo has successfully represented one of the largest multinationals in the food and beverage industry in their expansions throughout the State of Florida. He has acted as lead counsel in multimillionaire acquisitions of manufacturing plants, oil companies, financial institutions, broker dealers, real estate funds, master franchisors, and shopping centers.

    Mr. Salcedo has also designed corporate structures for the organization of US subsidiaries and branches of several international corporations in a variety of industries, including but not limited to food and beverages, oil and energy, banking, resorts and hospitality, money services, aviation and international courier. As part of the corporate planning, he designed structures that provide immigration benefits without leaving aside the corporate and tax implications of doing business in the United States.

    Mr. Salcedo has dual law licenses and a bicultural background, allowing him to provide nuanced counsel to foreign and domestic clients. He is licensed to practice law in the State of Florida and Venezuela.
